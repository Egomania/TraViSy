Traffic Visualization Making Network Traffic Understandable

Packet-based Traffic Visualization up to Layer 4.

Ethernet Traffic

Timing Diagram shows Ethernet addresses and sent Ethertype of messages.

Bar Chart shows Ethernettype Distribution among the dataset.

Forced Graph shows Ethernet addresses and connections between them.

Chord Chart shows Ethernet addresses and flows between them, as well as amount of traffic.

IP Traffic

D3 Forced Graph shows IP Traffic between different IP Addresses.

Binned Timelines show the amount of packets of sending and receiving IP-Addresses.

An Adjacency Matrix shows the communications relations between different IP Addresses.

Bar Charts show the TTL-Distribution and the Flag-Distribution of the selected Dataset.

Flows between IP Addresses are shown in a separate Graphs-Tab using VIS-, Sigma-, Alchemy- and plain D3-Libraries. .

TCP/UDP Traffic
