Timing

Timing Distribution shows sending Ethernet Addresses and Ethernettype of the sent packet.
Use Next- and Prev- Button to navigate through the data or choose a range of packets that has to be shown.
Hovering over the data shows more detailed information on the selected packet.


Ethernettype

Distribution of Ethernet-Type shows the distribution of Ethernettypes among sent messages.
Hovering over the data shows more detailed information.
The y-axis shows the number of packets of the specific type.
The x-axis shows the Ethernettype as hexadecimal representation.


Forced Diagramm

Communication Relations are shown in the forced diagramm.
Hovering over nodes shows the Ethernet address of the chosen node.
The thickness of the connecting lines imply the amount of traffic between the nodes.
The colors imply only-sending Node, only-receiving Node and sending-receiving Node.


Ethernet Communication

The Chord Chart shows the amount of traffic between Nodes.
Hovering over the diagram selects a specific Ethernet address.
The nodes shown in the diagram are only sending-receiving Nodes.
